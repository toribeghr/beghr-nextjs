## Typical Workflow

1. **Setup**: Navigate to your product codebase
2. **Planning**: Claude asks questions about your vision
3. **UI Replication**: Reuses your existing components
4. **Scene Creation**: Builds scenes showcasing features
5. **Enhancement**: Add voiceover, music, effects
6. **Iteration**: Refine based on feedback

## Best Practices

- **Use existing components**: Don't recreate - reuse your actual UI
- **Reference marketing materials**: Align with your messaging
- **Keep it simple**: Focus on key features, not everything
- **Founder perspective**: Show what you'd demo to a customer
- **Iterate**: First pass rarely perfect, plan for refinement

## Audio Enhancement

Consider adding:
- **Voiceover**: Explain features as they're shown
- **Background music**: Match your brand aesthetic
- **Sound effects**: Clicks, transitions, success sounds

Tools like ElevenLabs (via MCP) can generate all of these.

## Cost Considerations

High-quality product demos can require:
- 2-3 hours of time
- Significant AI tokens (especially with Opus 4.5)
- Audio generation credits (if using voiceover/music)

Plan accordingly for professional results.
